Masala dosa

Masala dosa, also called masale dosey, is a South Indian dish. It is a type of dosa and has its origin in the town of Udupi in Karnataka. It is made from rice, lentils, urad dal, chana dal, fenugreek, puffed rice, toor dal and dry red chilli, and served with potato curry, chutneys, and sambar.
