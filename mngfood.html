Mangalore's Specialty

Gujje Podi

Also known as 'Kadgi Podi', are delicious, munchy, hot and spicy, traditional Konkani deep-fried tender jackfruit chips.

Churmuri

Flattened rice (poha) and puffed rice (murmura) are the 2 forms of rice, commonly consumed in India along with rice in its original grain form. This Puffed Rice Snack or Churumuri is a popular street food from Karnataka in South India.

Golibaje

Golibaje or Mangalore bajji is an Indian fried food made from various flours and curd. In the Tulu Nadu region.

Kori Rotti

Kori rotti is a spicy dish of the Tulu Udupi-Mangalorean cuisine of India. It is a combination of red chili and coconut milk-based chicken curry and crisp, dry wafers made from boiled rice. Kori means chicken in Tulu.

Pathrode

Patrode/Patrodo/Patra/Patrodu is originally a vegetarian dish from India.

Anjal Fry

A restaurant-style delicacy of spicy fried Anjal fry served with delicious and tangy tawa masala infused with aromatic fried curry leaves.

Mangalore Buns

Mangalore buns are sweet fried bread made with mashed bananas, flour, sugar and spiced with a touch of ground cumin.
